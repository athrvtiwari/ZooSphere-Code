How It Works

  • First, you choose something and type it on the screen at the back.
  • You can type an animation to make the hologram move.
  • The hologram can be put in any style, such as, it can be colourful, monochrome, colours or just normal.
  • The Zoosphere can be turned off or on with the control panel (which is not connected), where you choose your object.
