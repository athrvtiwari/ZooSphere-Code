Our Philosophy

Everyday animals are being captured to be put in zoos. But have you ever thought of how they feel? We designed a machine that can save them all: the zoosphere. The zoosphere projects an interactive hologram as any image you can think of: small, big, living and nonliving. The zoosphere is a small, flying projector mounted on a drone. This allows the “hologram” to move and interact with viewers. The zoosphere can help animals be saved from captivity.
