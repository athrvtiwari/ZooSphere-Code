Our Connections

The United Nations have made sustainable development goals (SDGs) that are to be followed to make the world a better place. The ZooSphere contributes to sustainable development by being powered by solar panels. It also has a backup power system for emergencies. This aligns the ZooSphere with SDG 7: Clean and Affordable Energy. Additionally, the ZooSphere is connected to SDG 14: Life Below Water and SDG 15: Life on Land, as it projects animals for our entertainment visually instead of capturing them.

Round Square Heroes of Discovery Connected With Us

The ZooSphere is connected with Responsible Rami Sustainability Suki. Why Responsible Rami? Because, using ZooSphere means that you are responsible for helping the environment. Why Sustainability Suki? Because, Sustainable Suki is the round square hero related to protecting the environment and nature all around us. She is connected to the ZooSphere as the ZooSphere is powered by solar energy, which is clean and reusable energy, and it contributes to some of the SDGs.
